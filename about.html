More About Me!

I’m Chavi, a Graphic Designer based in Mumbai, passionate about helping brands stand out ✦

I’ve always been drawn to design in all its forms, never wanting to limit myself to one title or style. After months of hands-on experience at an agency, I discovered just how much I love bringing ideas to life. Every project excites me—especially the moment when I get to see my work printed and take it from screen to reality. Design is more than just my job; it's a journey, and I’m always eager to see where the creative process will take me next.

I believe in the power of fun-loving, vibrant design to bring brands to life and create memorable connections.
