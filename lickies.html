Lickies

Description

Lickies is a playful ice cream brand for dogs, offering 20 delicious and healthy flavors that bring joy and excitement to every pup. This project was about creating a fresh, vibrant visual identity that reflects the brand's fun-loving and energetic personality. The design features a lively color palette and quirky typography, bringing a sense of playfulness and vibrancy to every touchpoint. Each element is crafted to capture the joy and unique charm of Lickies, making it a brand that truly stands out while appealing to both dogs and their owners.
