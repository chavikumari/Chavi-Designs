DOMS

Description

Inspired by the iconic works of Vincent van Gogh, this academic project aimed to spark creativity in young artists. The campaign, centered around a Van Gogh-themed art competition, encouraged participants to explore their artistic potential. As part of this project, I contributed to the design of the limited-edition DOMS pencil color packaging, drawing inspiration from the vibrant hues of “Starry Night” and “Sunflowers.” Additionally, I designed various marketing materials, including brochures, posters, and social media graphics, to promote the competition and engage the target audience.
